What is a CTF?

Don't know what a computer is? Create a team and come anyways. The team can help you learn. A CTF, or Capture the Flag, is a jeopardy-style cybersecurity competition. Teams of 1-4 students compete against other teams to solve the most problems and fight their way to the top of the leaderboard.

A CTF can educate its participants in many areas, and we specifically design our problems to accommodate every experience level from beginner to advanced.
